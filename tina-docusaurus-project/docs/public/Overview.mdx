Bankuity is a financial intelligence platform designed to transform raw consumer bank data into actionable insights that drive profitability and smarter decision-making. At its core, Bankuity leverages proprietary Advanced Banking Verification (ABVâ„¢) technology to go beyond the static snapshots provided by traditional Instant Bank Verification (IBV).

By decoding transaction patterns in real-time, the platform empowers lenders, landlords, and financial institutions to mitigate risk while identifying new growth opportunities in underserved markets, such as the gig economy.

Key capabilities of Bankuity include:

| Feature                            | Description                                                                                                                               |
| :--------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Income Analysis & Verification** | Uses machine learning to identify and verify varied income sources, assess stability, and predict future cash flow.                       |
| **Risk Assessment**                | Delivers deep behavioral analytics and predictive modeling to evaluate repayment probability beyond traditional credit scores.            |
| **Lending Intelligence**           | Recommends optimal loan amounts, terms, and personalized debit schedules aligned to a customer's specific payday patterns.                |
| **Lifecycle Management**           | Provides insights across the entire customer journey from initial acquisition and underwriting to retention and collections optimization. |
